Research & Writing

I study International Security Policy and Technology, Media and Communications at Columbia University. I’m interested in the behaviors of bad actors online, using data to analyze networks, the digital divide, and technology and national security.

Digital Divide

Research on rural broadband and the digital divide in the U.S. and globally, as well as the naturally occurring digital inequities that arise from broadband infrastructure and investment

Technology and National Security

Research on China’s Dominance of 5G and U.S. national security concerns relating to siting and infrastructure expansion
